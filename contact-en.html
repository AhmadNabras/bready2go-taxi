1. What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Dilbeek, its deelgemeentes, Asse, Ternat, Aalst, and the surrounding towns. In addition to these areas, we provide transportation services throughout all of Belgium. If you need to travel beyond these regions, feel free to contact us, and we’ll do our best to accommodate your needs.

2. How long has Bready2go been in business?

Bready2go has been providing reliable and professional taxi services for 8 years, ensuring a smooth and stress-free experience for our customers.

3. Are you available 24/7?

Yes! We operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays. You can book a ride at any time, day or night.

4. How do I contact customer support?

You can contact us anytime by calling +32 487 99 18 77 or emailing us at Nabras.ahmad@gmail.com. We’re here to assist you with any inquiries or feedback.

5. How can I book a taxi?

You can easily book a ride by calling us at +32 487 99 18 77 or using our online booking form. We recommend booking in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak times.

6. Can I schedule a ride in advance?

Absolutely! You can schedule a ride in advance by contacting us directly. We’ll make sure a driver is ready for you at the scheduled time.

7. Do you accommodate special requests, such as child seats?

Yes, we offer child-friendly vehicles and can provide child seats upon request. Please let us know your requirements when booking your ride.
